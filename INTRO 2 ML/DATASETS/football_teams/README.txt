The dataset contains some general data on  English Premier League players scraped from https://scores.nbcsports.com/epl/rosters.asp
1_num - is a squad number of a player
2_Name - is unsorted Name and Surname of a player
3_Position - position of a player ([Goalkeeper, Defender, Midfielder, Forward])
4_Height - is a height of a player (in cm and inches / contains NaNs)
5_Weight - is a weight of a player (in kgs and lbs / contains NaNs)
6_BirthDate - is a birth date of a player (DD:MM:YYYY / contains NaNs)
7_Birthplace - birth place of a player
Team - a team player belongs to
